Anti-Spam Guide

Best practices for sending cold outreach campaigns successfully.

Recommended Settings (for 100+ leads)
  • Daily Limit: Keep it between 100 - 150.
  • Cooldown gap: Min 30s / Max 90s.
  • Batching: 5 mails/batch with 10 min break.
  • Randomize Cooldown: Always set to Yes to mimic human-sending.
Domain DNS Keys Setup
1. SPF Record Authorizes our SMTP server to send mail from your domain.
2. DKIM Signature Cryptographically signs the email to prove authentic origin.
3. DMARC Alignment Prevents domain spoofing and verifies SPF/DKIM authentication.
Content Optimization
  • Always use personalized variables (e.g. [[company_name]]) to make each email body unique.
  • Avoid words like "100% Free", "Guarantee", "Buy Now", "Earn Cash".
  • Verify email lists to avoid bounces (dead emails ruin sending reputation).
