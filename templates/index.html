How to Get Your App Password

Google requires third-party apps to use an isolated 16-character App Password rather than your standard Gmail account password. Here's exactly how to generate one in 60 seconds:

  1. 1
    Go to your Google Account Security page.
  2. 2
    Make sure 2-Step Verification is enabled.
  3. 3
    In the search bar at the top, type App Passwords and select it.
  4. 4
    Type an app name (e.g. DhruvBuilds Hub) and click Create.
  5. 5
    Copy the 16-letter password displayed and paste it into the form on the left.
🛡️ Your App Password is fully stored in your local encrypted SQLite database and only sent directly to Google's highly secure smtp.gmail.com SSL servers.
